Why is Team Pluto different?

Team PLUTO, a media relations company with the motto "Passion Leading Us to Optimism,"is dedicated to collaborating with individuals from all walks of life, bringing them into the digital realm and integrating them into the digital world. We are among the pioneers in realizing the vision of connecting Indian society through social media and making a complete digital India. Through simple strategies, we strive to achieve significant outcomes. Our expertise spans various fields, including sports and event photography, event management, celebrity management, public relations, digital promotions, and outdoor promotions across India. We are here to demonstrate to our clients that everything they envision can be achieved with the help of Team PLUTO.

About us

Founder R. Puagzh Murugan, an ex-journalist, transitioned into profiling sports photographer, covering all international matches across the country, including ICC, IPL, ISL, PVL, PBL, and PROKA in India for the past 8 years. Additionally, I 've published a book named "Tennis in Slow Motion,"with the first copy being presented to the Sports Minister, Udhaynidhi Stalin. I have also been awarded as the youngest sports photographer by Sporting Seeds PR Agency. I am also passionate about digital media, particularly the growth in digital realms in India over the last 10 years. Eventually, working on it led to more things, and later, I founded Team Pluto, a media relations company that specializes in photography, event management, outdoor promotions, digital promotions, public relations, and celebrity management. With vast experience, we have successfully covered big events like IPL and ISL, in movie promotions like "vendhu thanindhathu kaadu,""Comali,""Pithalamathi,"and did big audio launch events like "Doubletuckerr."In digital, we have promoted more than 30 films in the last 2 years alone, and many phenomenal works have been accomplished.
